Foundation Sixty6

Working in a small group and functioning as a mini-studio. level-4 students will design, develop and pitch a professional marketing and advertising campaign.

The mission of the foundation sixity6 is to facilitate improved access to mental healthcare for youth between the ages of 16 and 25 suffering from psychosis, bipolar disorder(BPD), and schizophrenia, specifically within Southwestern Ontario.
